About Us

Dog is man's bestfriend!

We, the students from BulSU LHS Senior Highschool, created this website to promote awareness on the current situation of Dog welfare here in the Philippines.

We hope that as you read and view our contents in this website, you'll be encourage to help us in our project, not because we want to, but because we have to, before it's too late.
