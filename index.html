VISION

Our vision is the welfare of the dogs in the Community. Dogs is a living being and need to be treated well. We need to act for them to be saved.

MISSION

Give a place for stray dogs to have a better life.

GOAL

Our goal is to save the dog welfare in the community.
Giving a better life for stray dogs/aspin is one of the best thing human beings can contribute to the community and to the world. 

OUR AWESOME TEAM

We, the students of Bulacan State University Laboratory Highschool SHS specializing in STEM strand, hope that this website will help us be more responsible and caring to the dogs in our community. May this website guide you and enlighten you on the problems we currently have in our community.
